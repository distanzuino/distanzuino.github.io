What's Distanzuino?

Where distance becomes music...

Distanzuino is an easily financeable, innovative digital musical instrument that functions based on distance measurement. The foundations of the device are Arduinos and ultrasonic sensors. The frequencies of the sounds can be influenced with these sensors. They measure the distance from the sensor to the object using ultrasound. This way, the frequency can be controlled with hands. Our goal is to playfully teach young people about music and technology, which is becoming increasingly important nowadays, with the help of Distanzuino.

Why is this musical instrument beneficial?

Distanzuino is an innovative musical instrument that is relatively inexpensive compared to other musical instruments. It is easily reconstructable and customizable by the user, making the use of the musical instrument much more personal and fun, which greatly facilitates learning the basics of technology.
